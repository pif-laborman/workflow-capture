## Executive Summary

**"Kiddlo" is not registered anywhere.** No exact match found in any database searched. However, the phonetically near-identical "KIDLOLAND" mark is **active and registered** by IDZ Digital in classes 9 and 41 — the exact classes a childcare platform would need. This creates a meaningful opposition risk. Attorney consultation recommended before filing.

## Risk Assessment

### Direct Conflict Risk: **Medium-High**

| Factor | Assessment |
|--------|-----------|
| Exact name "Kiddlo" registered? | No — clear |
| Phonetically similar mark active? | Yes — KIDLOLAND (classes 9, 41) |
| Same owner previously held "KIDLO"? | Yes — cancelled, but shows intent to protect the name family |
| Class overlap? | Yes — class 9 (software) and 41 (education) are directly relevant |
| Market overlap? | Partial — both target parents of young children, but Kidlo is apps/games, Kiddlo would be facility search |
| Geographic overlap? | Yes — KIDLOLAND is registered via WIPO/EUIPO, covers Czech Republic |

### Key Risk Scenarios

1. **Opposition at filing:** IDZ Digital could oppose a "Kiddlo" trademark application based on likelihood of confusion with KIDLOLAND. The 1-letter difference between "Kidlo" and "Kiddlo" is very small.

2. **Cease and desist:** Even without filing a trademark, using "Kiddlo" commercially could trigger a C&D letter from IDZ Digital's counsel if they monitor the space.

3. **Consumer confusion:** Parents searching for "Kidlo" educational apps could land on "Kiddlo" childcare platform and vice versa. Both serve parents of young children.

### Mitigating Factors

1. **Different service category:** Kidlo = educational apps/games. Kiddlo = childcare facility search/management. These are related but not identical services.

2. **KIDLO mark was cancelled:** The fact that the standalone "KIDLO" mark was declared invalid may weaken their opposition claim for "KIDDLO".

3. **Czech-specific platform:** Kiddlo would primarily serve Czech parents in Czech language. KidloLand is English-language global. Geographic and linguistic differentiation exists.

4. **Class 35/42 may be clear:** If Kiddlo registers in classes 35 (business management/advertising for facilities) and 42 (platform/SaaS) rather than 9 and 41, there may be less overlap.

## Recommendations

### Immediate Actions

1. **Consult a trademark attorney** (existing task, keep P2 or elevate to P1). Share this document as the briefing. Key question: does "Kiddlo" in classes 35/42 (facility platform/SaaS) create likelihood-of-confusion with "KIDLOLAND" in classes 9/41 (children's apps)?

2. **Do NOT register domains or build brand assets yet.** The "Secure kiddlo.cz" task should remain blocked on trademark clearance.

3. **Prepare 3-5 backup names** in case the attorney advises against "Kiddlo." See strategy review for initial suggestions (Skolkovo, NajdiSkolku.cz, Deticky.cz, MojeŠkolka.cz).

### Filing Strategy (if attorney gives green light)

- Register in **classes 35 and 42** first (less overlap with KidloLand)
- Consider whether class 41 is needed (education services) — this is where the highest conflict risk lies
- File in Czech Republic first (national mark), then consider EUIPO if expanding
- Monitor IDZ Digital's trademark activity via WIPO Madrid Monitor

### If Attorney Advises Against "Kiddlo"

- Already have backup names from strategy review
- Additional options worth exploring:
  - **Školička** (diminutive of "školka" — cute, very Czech)
  - **NajdiŠkolku.cz** (functional: "Find a kindergarten")
