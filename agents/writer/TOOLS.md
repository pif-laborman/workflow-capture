# TOOLS.md - Writer Agent

## Newsletter Format

Every edition MUST follow this structure:

```
SUBJECT: <subject line — compelling, not clickbait>
PREVIEW: <preview text, 40-90 characters>

## Hook
2-3 sentences that make them want to keep reading. Start with tension, a surprising fact, or a direct challenge to conventional wisdom.

## Main Story
400-500 words. One big idea with supporting evidence. Use the strongest angle from the research. Include specific numbers and source citations.

## Quick Hits
3 bullet-point stories from the remaining research angles. 2-3 sentences each. Each should be independently interesting.

## Takeaway
One actionable thing the reader can do this week. Specific, not vague. Include a clear CTA.
```

## ICP Context

Target reader: Heads of Ops, Supply Chain, Commercial at retail/e-commerce companies.
- They KNOW AI matters but feel behind
- They're overwhelmed by tools, hype, noise
- They want practical wins, not theory
- They want to look smart to their boss

Tagline energy: "Get simple AI wins before everyone else."

## Word Count

800-1000 words total. This is a hard constraint. Count before finishing.

## Output Rules

- Follow the exact format above
- Use plain text for KEY: value lines — no markdown bold, no asterisks around keys
- End with STATUS: done as the final line
- On revision: read editor feedback carefully, address every specific note, preserve what scored well, fix what scored poorly — do not rewrite from scratch

## On Revision

When editor feedback is present in the prompt, you are revising a previous draft. The editor scored your work on 6 categories and provided specific notes. Address each flagged issue. Keep what works. Fix what doesn't.
